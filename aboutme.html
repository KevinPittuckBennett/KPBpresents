About KPB Presents...

KPB Presents is led by Kevin Pittuck-Bennett, who brings a background in radio production, events hosting, content creation and 20 years working with community groups and charities through the co-op sector.

Having navigated career transitions personally, Kevin understands how important it is to support individuals and organisations in rediscovering their voice and sharing their stories effectively.

Experience & Expertise

Kevin has hosted panels, talks, and major events including the Pride of Essex Awards, and has written speeches, articles, and web content for a range of organisations. His approach is informed by a deep understanding of community, volunteering, and the importance of values-led work.

Advocacy & Media

Through the KPB Presents YouTube channel, Kevin shares videos and presentations on topics of personal and community significance, from his own journey through surrogacy and weightloss, travel guides, and social impact initiatives. Plus the occasional lighter piece for balance.

Values & Approach

Kevin works collaboratively with charities and organisations, producing content and promotion that amplifies their mission. Every project is approached with empathy, creativity, and a focus on helping people communicate authentically and effectively.
