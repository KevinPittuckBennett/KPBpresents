Let's talk.

"I work with individuals, charities, and organisations who want to communicate more clearly, more frequently, or with greater confidence. Sometimes that’s on camera, occasionally on stage, very often it is on the page. It nearly always starts with a one-to-one chat, figuring out what someone wants to say and how they want to say it" - Kevin

Voice & Video

Kevin creates and hosts interview-style videos that help people and groups tell their story in an interesting and personable way. Some work lives on the KPB Presents YouTube channel, others are created for the client to use on their own channels. View selected conversations →

Speaking & Events

Experienced in hosting radio, panels, talks, and events, Kevin focuses on clarity, warmth, and connection. With 20 years’ experience in the co-op sector, he is particularly adept at working with values-led organisations and community groups.

Writing

Kevin writes speeches, articles, and web content, shaping stories so they land clearly and honestly. His writing often supports videos or events, but can also stand alone as independent articles or content.

Community & Collaboration

Kevin collaborates with charities and organisations supporting people through change, producing content and promotion that amplifies their work. Projects may be voluntary or collaborative, focussing on impact, visibility, and giving a voice to those who are often unheard.
